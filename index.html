How It Works
The science behind our color-shifting stickers, simplified.
01
🔬
Leuco Dye Technology
Our stickers use microencapsulated leuco dyes that reversibly change their molecular structure with temperature.
02
🌡️
Threshold Activation
Each product has a calibrated activation temperature — color changes are sharp, visible, and repeatable.
03
🔄
Fully Reversible
As temperature returns to baseline, the sticker reverts to its original color — no degradation over time.
04
✅
Safe & Certified
Food-contact safe inks (FDA/EU compliant). Washable up to 40°C for fabric patches.
